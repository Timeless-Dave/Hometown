Visit Iseyin

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Oyo.

Top three activities to do at Iseyin

Attend a show or a concert

The Iseyin Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a picnic in the local park

The Iseyin Park is perfect for picnic, getting some air, or just chilling out.

Play sports at the Lilleborg field

Play volleyball, football, basketball or lift some weights.
